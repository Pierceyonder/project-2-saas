What is SpeakEasy?

Get ready for your travel adventure with SpeakEasy. SpeakEasy provides an easy, fun, and unique learning experience that will quickly evolve your language and speaking skills.

SpeakEasy offers learning programs in many different languages, including Spanish, French, and German. Depending on the level of your membership, you can learn several different languages all at once or dedicate yourself to just one.

Our programs offer different levels of learning depending on your needs; need to strike up a conversation? Need a more formal approach? SpeakEasy has you covered and more.

Tutoring

SpeakEasy also offers private tutoring lessons for those in need of more individualized training. Our certified language trainers can help boost your linguistic skills even further and faster.
